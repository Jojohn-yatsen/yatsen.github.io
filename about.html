是的，因為1980年代臺北鐵路地下化工程，梅屋敷主體建物經過遷移重組，往東北移動了50公尺左右；戶外庭園則改為中式園林的設計，因此不是當年日治時期的樣貌。 That’s right — during the Taipei Railway Underground Project in the 1980s, the main structure of Umeyashiki was dismantled and relocated about 50 meters to the northeast. The surrounding garden area was later redesigned in the style of a traditional Chinese landscape garden, so it no longer reflects the original appearance from the Japanese colonial period.
