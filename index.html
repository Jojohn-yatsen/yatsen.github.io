臺灣古寫真上色答復： Response from Colorized old photos of Taiwan:

• 這批圖資整體狀況欠佳，修復難度極高，以「基隆火車站前荷揚狀況」來說，雖然網點化不算特別嚴重，但其實是沒什麼細節的，搬運物品的工人有沒有戴帽子都難以辨識；畫面右邊的新店街候船室是重要的視覺焦點，進行修復就需要很多人工介入，例如找其他可以補的圖來補，但這些圖光線、角度不見得會和原圖一致，就需要人工大量介入調整修飾。

•此外在黑白原圖中難以辨識的戎克船 （昔日臺灣常見的船隻，配色以紅黑綠藍白為主）、基隆郵便局（今愛三路基隆郵局）、日新橋（今富狗橋），都是1913年在這個基隆港視角會看到的重點景觀，在修復後得以重現細節，則是來自於對於歷史場景的專業理解和詮釋。

• 本次是以長邊4000-5000px以上為目標去修補，日後如果辦理實體展覽需要大圖輸出，也會有非常好的視覺效果，建築的風格特徵等細節看得更清楚，我們也可以更貼近當年這位革命旅人來臺所見的情景！ • The overall condition of these images is poor, making restoration extremely difficult. Taking the image of "Unloading Scene in Front of Keelung Railway Station" as an example, while the dot matrix effect isn't particularly severe, it lacks detail; it's difficult to even discern whether the workers carrying goods are wearing hats. The waiting room on Xindian Street on the right side of the image is a key visual focal point, requiring significant manual intervention for restoration. This might involve finding other images to supplement the originals, but these images may not have the same lighting and angles, necessitating extensive manual adjustments and retouching.

• This restoration effort aims to restore images with a long side of 4000-5000 pixels or more. This will provide a much better visual experience for future physical exhibitions requiring large-scale output, allowing for clearer visualization of architectural styles and details, and enabling us to more closely understand the scene witnessed by this revolutionary traveler during his visit to Taiwan!
